ABOUT US

Coronavirus has triggered a funding crisis for NGOs when they are needed most.

The economic downturn means future aid budgets and donations are likely to decline.

A liquidity fund could help NGOs cover overheads in the short term.

In these unprecedent times when COVID-19 contines to spread and to impact almost every individual and organization
across the world directly or indirectly ,non-profit organizations or non-government organization(NGOs) are also deeply affected now
and in times to come.As it is not clear what the current pandemic situation holds in future,it is important to balance the scale between panic and carelessness.

Preparedness is the key.
